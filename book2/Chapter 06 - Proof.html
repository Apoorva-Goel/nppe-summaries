Burden & Standard of Proof

Who proves it?

In tort and breach-of-contract suits, the plaintiff generally bears the burden of proving the case against the defendant.

To what standard?

Balance of probabilities — more likely than not (>50%). Note this is lower than the criminal standard of beyond a reasonable doubt.

Engineers as Expert Witnesses

Engineers are often called to give expert evidence. The expert's duty is to assist the court with objective, independent opinion within their area of expertise — not to advocate for the party that retained them.

Remember: the standard is "more likely than not," and an expert's credibility rests on objectivity, not partisanship.
