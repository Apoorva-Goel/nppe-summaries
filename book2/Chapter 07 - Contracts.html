Freedom of contract: if a "bad business deal" is negotiated, courts will not rewrite it with more favourable terms. The bargain is the parties' to make.

Two More Concepts

Assignment

Contractual benefits (e.g. the right to be paid) can be assigned to a third party — but generally burdens/obligations cannot be unloaded without consent.

Good faith — Bhasin v Hrynew (2014, SCC)

A new organizing principle: parties must perform contracts honestly — not lie or knowingly mislead about matters linked to performance.
