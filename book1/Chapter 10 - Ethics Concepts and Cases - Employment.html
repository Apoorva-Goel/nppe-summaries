When Your Employer Overrules You — flowchart

1 · Explain your advice in writing — and the consequences of ignoring it
▼
2 · Get a written response — ensure they understand (esp. non-technical managers)
▼
3 · If the act is illegal/unsafe → refuse, and escalate / report as required
The bright line: employment is a contract and employers have authority — but if asked to do something illegal or that endangers the public, the professional must advise it's improper and refuse. The duty to public safety overrides employer instructions. Document everything in writing.
