The 7 Competencies — what you must demonstrate

Apply knowledge

Engineering knowledge, methods & techniques.

Use tools

Engineering tools, technology & equipment.

Protect the public interest

The paramount competency.

Manage activities

Plan & manage engineering work.

Communicate

Convey engineering information clearly.

Collaborate + keep learning

Work in the Canadian environment & maintain/enhance skills.

Practical tip: start documenting your experience the day you start working — detailed, competency-mapped records make licensing far smoother. Apply for the internship as soon as you graduate.
