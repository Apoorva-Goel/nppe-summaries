Extra Requirements for Serving the Public

Firms need authorization

All provinces (except Quebec) require firms (companies/partnerships/corporations) to hold an additional permit — e.g. PEO's Certificate of Authorization.

Insurance

Professional liability insurance is commonly required or strongly advised — some Associations mandate it.

"Consulting Engineer" title

A reserved designation: in Ontario requires a Certificate of Authorization + 5 years' experience beyond licensing.

NPPE focus: an individual P.Eng can usually serve the public, but a firm needs a Certificate of Authorization. The "Consulting Engineer" title is restricted and carries extra experience requirements.
