Continuing Professional Development — ongoing learning & skill maintenance.

Compliance, reporting & recording of CPD activities.

Periodic review of members' practice.

4 · Guidelines

Practice guidelines & standards to follow.

Mandatory & Varied

Recommended mandatory

Engineers Canada recommends programs be mandatory, with sanctions for members who won't participate.

Requirements differ

Programs vary widely by Association — e.g. APEGA, APEGBC, APEGS, PEO (historically a voluntary annual report), OIQ, and others each publish their own CPD guideline.

Engineer's takeaway: CPD is a career-long professional obligation, not optional self-improvement. Know your province's specific CPD requirements, keep records, and follow the practice guidelines — competence is part of protecting the public.
