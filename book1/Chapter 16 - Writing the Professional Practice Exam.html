▼
Step 2 · Begin work experienceUsually need ~2 years of experience before writing the PPE; apply to write once eligible

▼
Step 4 · Complete experience & get licensedFinish the ~4-year experience requirement, pay the fee → P.Eng / P.Geo

Exam Logistics

If you don't appear

Failing to show is generally treated the same as writing and failing — and you forfeit the fee.

If you fail

You must rewrite the exam.

Format varies

Each Association sets its own PPE/NPPE format under its Act.

This is your exam: the PPE/NPPE tests professional practice, law & ethics — exactly the material in this book. Get your Association's official study materials, study independently, and apply to write as soon as you're eligible (≈2 years' experience).
