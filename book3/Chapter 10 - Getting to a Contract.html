Project Delivery Methods — who does conceive / design / build / operate / finance

Design-Bid-Build

Owner/consultant designs & inspects; a separate contractor builds. Most common in North America. Design risk stays with the owner.

Construction Management

Owner/consultant designs & inspects; CM coordinates trades. Owner retains more risk & control.

Design-Build

One entity designs AND builds — single point of responsibility.

Design-Build-Operate

Adds operation to the builder's scope.

DBO-Finance

Adds financing — the basis of many P3 arrangements.

Common to all

Every project = conceive → design → construct → inspect → finance → operate.

Risk & obligation transfer: the chosen delivery method reallocates design, performance, cost, schedule and operating risk among the parties. Each variable can be wholly or partly shifted by the contract — and the party taking a risk prices it in. Match the structure to who can best manage each risk.
