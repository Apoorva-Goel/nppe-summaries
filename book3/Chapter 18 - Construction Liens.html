Construction Liens

Security for those who improve land but haven't been paid — every province has a statute.

Why Liens Exist

On a project, money flows lender → owner → general contractor → subs, workers & suppliers. Because payment follows performance, those down the chain risk doing work and not getting paid. The lien gives them security against the improved land so they're willing to supply labour & materials.

Who May Claim — and Who May Not

Can claim a lien

Workers, contractors, subcontractors, and material suppliers who supplied to the improvement and weren't paid.

Typically cannot

Subconsultants are often excluded under the statutory definitions — entitlement varies by province's specific wording.
