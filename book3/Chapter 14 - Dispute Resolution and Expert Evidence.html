Four ways to resolve a dispute — and how the litigation machine actually works.

Four Methods — voluntary ↔ imposed · binding ↔ non-binding

Negotiation

  • Purely voluntary
  • Parties control outcome
  • Non-binding until agreed

Mediation

  • Neutral facilitator
  • Before or after a dispute
  • Non-binding

Arbitration

  • Voluntary at contract stage
  • Private adjudicator
  • Binding award

Litigation

  • The court system
  • Public, slower, costly
  • Binding judgment

Expert Evidence — the engineer's role

Engineers, architects & geoscientists are often retained as expert witnesses to give opinion evidence within their expertise. The expert's overriding duty is to assist the court objectively — independence and impartiality, not advocacy, are what give the opinion weight.
