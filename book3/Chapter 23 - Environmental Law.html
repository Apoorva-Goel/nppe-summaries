Environmental Audits

Protect people

Safeguard employees & the public from contaminants.

Reduce liability

Limit claims from tenants/customers; cut remediation cost.

Build a defence

Help establish due-diligence defences to statutory offences.

Engineer's takeaway: environmental statutes mix offences, compensation, and proactive prevention. The due-diligence defence is central — documenting reasonable care (audits, ESAs, compliance systems) can be the difference between liability and acquittal.
