What Makes a Contract Voidable

Defective consent

Duress, misrepresentation, mistake, unconscionability — consent wasn't truly free or informed.

Performance impossible

Frustration / impossibility — events make performance radically different or impossible.

Consideration matters

Courts emphasize it: a promise to do what you're already obliged to do is not fresh consideration (Smith v Dawson, 1923).
